Last updated: 7 May 2026 Version: 1.0
beebeeb.io (InitLabs B.V.) uses a limited number of sub-processors and external service providers to deliver its service. This page contains the current list.
This list is binding for our Data Processing Agreement (Article 6 and Annex 3) and is updated when sub-processors are added, replaced or removed. We inform our business customers at least thirty (30) days in advance of changes, so they have the opportunity to object.

Objection to sub-processors
Business customers can object to a new or replaced sub-processor within fourteen (14) days after our notification. You may direct objections to legal@beebeeb.io. We will discuss the objection by mutual agreement. If we cannot find a solution, you have the right to terminate the agreement for the part that cannot reasonably be continued without the relevant sub-processor, in accordance with Article 6.3 of our Data Processing Agreement.
Contact
Questions about this list or about a specific sub-processor? Please contact us at legal@beebeeb.io or privacy@beebeeb.io.
