Not classified as sub-processors

The following systems are not sub-processors because they are operated in-house or self-hosted by beebeeb.io within its own infrastructure in the EEA:

  • Transactional email — self-hosted within EEA infrastructure; no third-party email provider is used
  • Customer support — handled internally; no external helpdesk tool is used
  • Accounting and bookkeeping — handled internally; no external accounting software or accountant processes personal data
  • Matomo Analytics (self-hosted in EEA) — we do not share analytics data with third parties
  • Authentication and session management — own system
  • Key derivation services — performed on the user's device (Zero-Knowledge)

Sub-contractors of sub-processors

Objection to sub-processors

Business customers can object to a new or replaced sub-processor within fourteen (14) days after our notification. You may direct objections to legal@beebeeb.io. We will discuss the objection by mutual agreement. If we cannot find a solution, you have the right to terminate the agreement for the part that cannot reasonably be continued without the relevant sub-processor, in accordance with Article 6.3 of our Data Processing Agreement.
